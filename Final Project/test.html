About Us

The country is known for producing some of the best dishes in Africa, representing a true fusion of Portuguese, Arab and African influences. It is also famous for its seafood, including prawns, lobsters, crabs and squid, which are highly appreciated by visitors. Local dishes along the coast have a charm that makes them unique and even more appetizing compared to food from neighboring countries, when ingredients such as coconut and piri-piri are used.

Upshwa or xima is one of the favorite side dishes and is made from corn or cassava flour and served with fish, meat or vegetables. One of Mozambique's most famous specialties is mathapa, a dish made from cassava leaves cooked in peanut sauce and usually prepared with crab or shrimp.



Most restaurants along the coast cook seafood at affordable prices, especially lobster, crayfish, grilled prawns and squid that can be stuffed, grilled or fried. Typical Dishes

Get to know a little about Mozambican gastronomy and some dishes that are not of Mozambican origin but are always present on our tables.

Badjias

Badjias are fried snacks, typical of Indian cuisine, but also very popular in Mozambique, where they are normally made with Nhemba Bean flour and sold on the street along with bread, to serve as "matabicho"

Cafreal Chicken

Cafreal Chicken is typical of the province of Inhambane. It has nothing special other than the name.

crab curry

The crab curry This seafood is prepared with legumes and vegetables. The recipe is very typical in the southern part of Mozambique.

Grilled fish

Grilled fish Made with any type of fish. It can go with potatoes, salads or even rice.

Mucapata

Mucapata is a typical dish from the Province of Zambézia, in the central region of Mozambique, prepared with rice, beans and coconut. A delicious recipe that bears cultural marks of the Mozambican people, specifically the Chuabo ethnic group from Zambézia.

Pumpkin coconut

The Pumpkin coconut is a typical culinary sweet. Pumpkin is a fruit originating from the American continent, being important in the diet of pre-Columbian civilizations.

Xiguinha

The Xiguinha Xiguinha is a typical dish from the southern region of Mozambique, which for generations has been telling stories of joy and resilience about the wonderful Mozambican people.

Zambezian chicken

Zambezian chicken It is a dish prepared with chicken and coconut. It is typical of the province of Zambézia.
